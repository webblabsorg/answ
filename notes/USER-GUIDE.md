## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit the Answly homepage
2. Click "Get Started" or "Register"
3. Enter your email, name, and password
4. Click "Create Account"
5. You'll be automatically logged in

### Logging In

1. Click "Login" on the homepage
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

## Taking Tests

### Browsing Exams

1. After logging in, click "Browse Exams" from the dashboard
2. You'll see available exams (GRE, SAT, GMAT, etc.)
3. Each card shows:
   - Exam name and category
   - Duration
   - Number of sections
   - Total questions

### Starting a Test

1. Click on an exam card to view details
2. Review the exam structure and sections
3. Click "Start Full Test" to begin
4. The timer will start automatically

### During the Test

**Navigation:**
- Use the question grid on the right to jump between questions
- Click "Next" to move to the next question
- Click "Previous" to go back

**Answering Questions:**
- **Multiple Choice:** Click on one option
- **Multiple Select:** Check all correct answers
- **Numeric Input:** Type your numerical answer
- **Text Input:** Type your text answer
- **True/False:** Select True or False
- **Essay:** Write your response in the text area

**Test Features:**
- **Flag Questions:** Click the flag icon to mark questions for review
- **Timer:** Visible at the top, showing remaining time
- **Progress:** See how many questions you've answered
- **Navigation Grid:** Shows answered (blue), flagged (yellow), unanswered (gray)

### Submitting Your Test

1. Answer all questions (or as many as you can)
2. Review flagged questions
3. Click "Submit Test" when ready
4. Confirm your submission
5. You'll be redirected to your results

## Tips for Success

### Before the Test

1. **Familiarize Yourself:** Take a practice test to understand the format
2. **Set Goals:** Know your target score
3. **Find a Quiet Space:** Minimize distractions during the test
4. **Check Your Internet:** Ensure stable connection

### During the Test

1. **Read Carefully:** Don't rush through questions
2. **Skip and Return:** If stuck, flag and move on
3. **Watch the Time:** Keep an eye on the timer
4. **Check Your Work:** Review answers before submitting

### After the Test

1. **Review Explanations:** Understand why answers are correct/incorrect
2. **Identify Patterns:** Look for recurring mistakes
3. **Focus on Weak Areas:** Practice topics where you scored low
4. **Take More Tests:** Practice makes perfect!

### Study Strategies

**For Time Management:**
- Take timed practice tests regularly
- Learn to pace yourself
- Don't spend too long on any one question

**For Content Mastery:**
- Review explanations thoroughly
- Make notes on difficult concepts
- Focus on your weak topics
- Take section-specific practice

**For Test Day:**
- Get plenty of rest the night before
- Eat a healthy meal before the test
- Arrive early (for in-person tests)
- Stay calm and confident

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I pause a test?

Not currently. Tests must be completed in one sitting. Plan accordingly!

### How is my score calculated?

- Each correct answer earns 1 point
- Scores are scaled to the exam's scoring system
- No penalty for incorrect answers

### Can I retake a test?

Yes! You can take each exam multiple times to track your improvement.

### What happens if I lose internet connection?

Your progress is saved automatically. If you lose connection, refresh the page and your answers will be restored.

### How long do I have access to my results?

Your results are saved permanently. You can review them anytime from your dashboard.

### Can I download my results?

Currently, results are viewable online only. Export features may be added in the future.
